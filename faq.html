  • How can I make an appointment?

  • Do you accept insurance?

    Dr. Juboori does not accept insurance and is not an in network provider for any insurance company. Upon request, Dr. Juboori can provide a receipt/superbill for you to submit to your insurance carrier, which may reimburse some of the fee.

  • What type of payment do you accept?

    Payment by cash, check, or PayPal, is due at each appointment unless other arrangements have been made in advance.

  • What is your cancellation policy?

    Appointments and initial consultations cancelled or rescheduled at least 48 hours before your appointment time will not be charged. Appointments and initial consultations cancelled or rescheduled within 48 hours before your appointment time will be charged a 50% fee. Missed appointments will be charged 100% of the appointment fee.
