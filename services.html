Top Quality Services

Comprehensive Evaluation

During the initial consultation, Dr. Juboori will be asking a variety of questions to better understand the patient's issues and perspective. It may also be useful to gather information from other individuals who know the patient well, such as family members, significant others, and teachers. After all the information is gathered, Dr. Juboori will present a detailed formulation of the patient's presenting problem and discuss treatment recommendations specifically tailored to the patient's needs. Treatment may include medications, psychotherapy, or a combination of the two.

Medication Management

If medications are indicated, Dr. Juboori will collaborate with the patient (and parents/guardians for minors) to determine the best option(s) to address the patient's individual needs. Once medications are started, follow-up appointments will be necessary to monitor the patient's progress and assess for side effects. Dr. Juboori maintains a conservative approach when using medications, particularly with children, and will prescribe them only when clinically appropriate.
By having the ability to provide both psychotherapy and medication management, I feel that patients will have more comprehensive treatment addressing issues from multiple perspectives.
